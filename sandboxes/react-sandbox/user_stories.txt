# User Stories

## Upon visiting the site a user can:
- view a list of UHL1 restaurants
	- by Union status: (wall-to-wall, partly Union)
## With one click a user can:
- nav to opentable to make a reservation at a particular restaurant
- place a call to a particular restaurant
- get directions to the restaurant
- view more information about a restaurant
## Viewing the restaurant info a user can:
- view a picture related to the restaurant
- view the street address of the restaurant
- click a link to visit the restaurant's website
- click a link to visit the yelp review for the restaurant?
